# dual channel

I have 12 gb of ram. It is more than plenty I'd say, but I *do* get weird OOM crashes every so often. \
Then, how do we comprise 12 gb of ram, considering it's all in powers of 2? \
You take 4+2+4+2, filling out the 4 ram sticks.

Now, that's not *awful* necessarily but it's kinda bad still. It's better to fill the first and third (or second and fourth) slots only, with higher capacity sticks, than fill out all of them with lower capacity. \
